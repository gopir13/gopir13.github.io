Stakeholder Interview

Project Vision

What is your vision for this offering?

A centralized attendance system to make the time logging easier for our employees globally with mood analysis.

How will you, personally, define success for this project?

If we can make attendance part of employees daily routine.

What worries you about this project? What’s the worst thing that could happen?

Employees might not value this product or find it hard to use.

What should this project accomplish for the business?

Timesheets offer a lot of value for a seemingly simple application.

  • Improved Workflow
  • Improved Task Management
  • Calculate Employees Cost
  • Effective Cost Management
  • Accurate Billing

Value Proposition

What problems do users have that this offering solves?

It enables the remote and on-site employees to register the attendance.

What is the core value prop of the offering?

Make the employees connected with the organization without geographical restrictions.

What are the main marketing messages?

Effortlessly track time and bill your clients for the hours you spend on their projects.
