It was during the summer of 2015 – The time when we all worked hard and each employee had a dream of their own, the startup we worked at was paradise for many, especially for the three of us.

Suriya Bala

With so many SaaS products flooding into market lately, this particular product has caught the eye of the startup community recently. Startup Mafia claims to be a “life support” product, mainly for startups and also for common people.

Most startups fail mainly due to the lack of market research and market segment. The start is always hard they say, especially when it come to a startup. But Startup mafia looks at a future where startups can ensure and analyze the depth and behavior of their market before stepping in.

A product with vision and seemingly strong team.

Suriya, being a business enthusiast has worked in real-time products from his college days. Suriya joined hands with Gopi and Sibi. Gopi, the product designer has worked in two successful products. Sibi, is the developer of the product and he is from the research and development team of a top MNC. Caught by the interest of the product, Goutham joined the team as a content writer. Goutham is a literature and public speaking enthusiast handling the content for Startup Mafia.

Startup Mafia, currently a four member team is a club of four different talents. The team is determined to bring about change in the startup community in the near future. Look out for more from Startup Mafia.
